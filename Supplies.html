In need of supplies?

Receiving Supplies

Mukilteo Library
We will post certain dates where we will be at the Mukilteo Library distributing supplies! These dates may vary in the beginning, but we will post on our website and social media!
Drop-off
If you cannot stop by the Mukilteo Library on those certain days, but you still want some art supplies, we may be able to drop off the supplies to you!
